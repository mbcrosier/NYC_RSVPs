NYC Restaurant Reservation Timing Guide
Reservation Guidance & Caveats
  • Restaurants regularly change their reservation schedules, so the information above is subject to change. See something that's out of date, or have a restaurant you think we should add? Let us know.
  • In many cases, online reservations are not the only option. Many restaurants reserve tables for walk-ins, set aside a portion of reservations for callers, or offer early access through the Amex Global Dining program (among others). Just because there aren't reservations online doesn't mean you can't go!
  • As of right now, the 'earliest reservation' date doesn't account for time of day, and time sorts alphabetically vs. by time. These fixes are coming soon.
